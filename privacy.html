Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

HealthPath Academy uses Firebase services to provide account login, progress syncing, hosting, and daily lesson updates.

Information We Store

When you create an account, we may store your email address, display name, profile photo from Google sign-in, lesson progress, quiz scores, bookmarks, private notes, and last-opened lesson. This data is used to make your learning experience work across devices.

Authentication

Sign-in is handled by Firebase Authentication. If you use Google sign-in, Google provides basic account information needed to identify your account.

Database

Your progress is stored in Cloud Firestore under your user account. Firestore security rules are designed so signed-in users can access their own user document, and course lesson content can be read by signed-in users.

Analytics And Hosting

The site may use Firebase/Google services that collect technical information such as device, browser, page, and performance data. This helps operate and improve the website.

Health Information

Do not enter protected health information, patient names, medical record numbers, or private patient details into notes. HealthPath is a learning platform, not a clinical record system.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact page and include only general account information, not private medical details.
